Commands:

00xxxxxx xxxxxxxx 			A = <constant> sign extended to 16 bits
11xxxxxx  					Commands

COPY 		Copy A to B
ANMRAT		A Frames per animation (0 stop)
MOVRAT 		A Frames per movement (0 stop)
ANMCLR  	Clear animations
ADDA 		add animation A to animation 
WAIT 		loop until A = 0
END 		end of script
HIDE 		Hide sprite
MOVE 		move to A,B
